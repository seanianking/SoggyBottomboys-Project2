About Us

This website was created as a project for the University of Utah Coding Bootcamp. It was created by the four of us in collaboration using HTML, CSS(Bootstrap), Jquery, Node.js, MySQL, Express and GitHub. If you like what you see, reach out to us. We want to hear from you and would be happy to answer any questions or respond to any comments you might have.
